About Me

When I started college my first semster of engineering was primarily coding, and I had to apply that knowledge in a group project in the second semester. I found that I really enjoyed coding, and when engineering didn't work out and I entered the workforce that stuck with me. As a machinist I was asked to take over programing the robotic arms they used to load and unload machines, and it finally occured to me that I could make a career out of this. I consulted with friends that had gone through coding bootcamps and entered the field, and once I had saved up enough to make the switch I started at We Can Code IT. Now that I have completed the We Can Code IT cohort, I am hoping to find a coding career that will allow me to apply my knowledge while continuing to grow as a developer.
